\# Habiton — Sample Q\&A Flow (5+ Exchanges)



\## Exchange 1

User: I can’t focus today.

Bot: Totally normal. Let’s not force it — what’s the ONE task you want to finish most?



\## Exchange 2

User: I need to study.

Bot: Good. Which subject, and how much time do you have right now?



\## Exchange 3

User: Python, 1 hour.

Bot: Perfect. We’ll do a 25–5–25 plan. First 25 mins: theory + notes. Next 25 mins: solve 2 questions. Want me to set the steps?



\## Exchange 4

User: Yes.

Bot: Step 1: open your notes. Step 2: write 5 key points. Step 3: solve 2 questions. Start with step 1 and tell me when done.



\## Exchange 5

User: Done.

Bot: Nice. You just broke the procrastination barrier. Next step: write your 5 key points. Keep it simple.



\## Exchange 6

User: I’m feeling better now.

Bot: That’s how focus works — it comes AFTER starting. You did the hardest part.
